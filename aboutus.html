ABOUT US




At Ranjit’s Gym, our goal is simple - We’re here to help you Know Your Own Strength. Here you’ll find an energetic, supportive environment full of all kinds of people who are committed to achieving their goals. So we create an environment where you can be at ease, feel comfortable asking for help and just do your own thing without ever having to worry about being judged no matter what your workout goals are.



Here at Ranjit's gym we have quite experience in this field because it was one of the few gyms set up in Ludhiana in year 2000. Our members derive benfits from our expert advice, personalized supervision, result-oriented approach. We offer a diverse set of services including gym, swimming, aerobics, steam bath, sauna bath and health counselling.
